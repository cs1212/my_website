YouTube Comment Sentiment Analysis

Tools: Python, TextBlob (NLP library), matplotlib (visualization)

GitHub for project

Ever wonder if Republicans are more toxic than Democrats or vice versa? Well wonder no more!

I scraped comments from various Republican and Democratic news channels covering the same news story and did some data cleaning before performing text analysis to determine how toxic comments are for each channel.

Contrary to initial beliefs, I discovered that the toxicity of each political party was relatively equal no matter the platform and from that, I concluded that no political party is more toxic than the other. Hatred is in the eye of the beholder.

Reddit Posts Data Anaysis

Tools: Python, PRAW (reddit api wrapper), matplotlib (visualization)

GitHub for project

For this project, my group and I wanted to know when the best time to post was in order to get the most upvotes and comments. More specifically, we looked at r/Funny since we believed that a person would post with the intention of wanting lots of upvotes and comments in this subreddit.

I collected 100 posts at midnight of each day and followed those posts for 24hrs, tracking the upvotes and comments every 30 minutes. In hindsight, if we wanted to improve this project, it would be better to collect new posts in addition to the previous ones every hour.

After analyzing the data, it appears that Thursday 9am-12pm had the highest rate of upvotes/comments being given in r/Funny.

Traitor Chess

Tools: JavaScript, HTML

GitHub for project

Chess site link to play

Traitor Chess is chess with a twist! You never know who's going to betray you and taking that to heart, I designed and coded Traitor Chess using JavaScript and HTML.

Traitor Chess follows classic chess rules except after a set number of turns, one random piece from both black and white side will change color, hence the 'traitor' name.

Because of the 'traitor', classic chess strategies do not work well in this game. I hope this will bring more interesting games and that you'll have fun playing!
